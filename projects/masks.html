Majora's Mask: part splitting for oversized geometry

The mask exceeded my printer's build volume. The solution: split the model into sections at natural seam lines, print separately, and join with registration features to ensure alignment. Painted after assembly. The challenge is planning the splits so the seams are invisible in the final result.
